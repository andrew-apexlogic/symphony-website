Conception to Award

Soliciting the Master Contract through Symphony - Proposal Intake Benefits

  • Contractors can register with their SAM Unique Entity Identifier (UEI), enhancing accuracy for all.
  • Quick insights through dashboard and reporting.
  • Audition manages roles and system security for contractors.
  • Audition validates proposal content to prevent incomplete submissions.
  • Audition warns contractors if submissions fail to meet requirements.
  • Audition organizes proposals so that intake is complete at close.
  • Contractors can view real-time validation and auto-calculated scores.
  • Apex Logic provides Q&A, training, and help desk support.
  • Q&A, submissions, clarifications, and evaluator data are centralized.

Evaluating Across the Master Contract - Source Selection Benefits

  • Symphony is configured to streamline the approval workflow of evaluations through to the award, based on the Source Selection Plan outlined by the Program.
  • Proposals are organized and ready for evaluation in Symphony according to the evaluation criteria.
  • Auto-calculation of scoring/evaluation methods for easy organization—"rack and stack"—enhancing the efficiency and integrity of the evaluation process.
  • All evaluation data is captured within Symphony, fully aligned with the solicitation requirements.
  • Transparency is ensured as the evaluation team sees exactly what the offeror submitted in context, including all data, scoring information, and any warnings or validation messages.
  • Supporting documents provided by bidders are linked to the relevant sections within their proposals, allowing evaluators to quickly access the evidence.
  • Bidders "tag" these documents with keywords using standard PDF notation before uploading them into Symphony.

Expediting the Award Phase - Determination Benefits

  • All evaluation data is collected and stored in a centralized system for easy access.
  • Symphony Audition generates PDF documents as needed for quick reference.
  • Audition's automation approach saves the Government Contracting Officer significant time by eliminating the need to manually create debriefs or gather documents from multiple sources or databases.
  • Audition verifies socio-economic status and vendor data.
  • Awardees can expect training and help desk support in tandem with program officials.
