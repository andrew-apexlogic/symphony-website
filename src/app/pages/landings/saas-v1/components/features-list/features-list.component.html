Solicitation Configuration

Symphony Audition is configured to meet the specific requirements of your program. During the creation of the solicitation, Apex Logic works with the government program team to ensure Symphony Audition collects the required information in a consistent format.

Proposal Intake

Offerors use the Symphony Audition product to respond to the solicitation. Audition accounts for the specific needs of your program based on how it was configured. Offerors respond to the solicitation by submitting information in a consistent format, enabling business intelligence and program efficiency. The system provides real-time validation to offerors and displays an error summary page. While Symphony will not reject a submission due to a validation error, it will notify both the program and the offeror of the error.

Offer Evaluation

The Audition module allows the program team to review offeror responses and supporting documentation by breaking down the solicitation into manageable sections. The evaluation is done consistently, allowing for fair competition.

Source Selection

The source selection process begins with the offeror and is configured at the master contract level to ensure fair evaluations for all proposals. The program's Source Selection Plan establishes roles and permissions, while validations alert both the offeror and source selection team of proposal errors. The entire process is documented in Symphony for Final Determinations, delivering a sorted list of the most qualified offerors based on your specifications.

Determinations

The final determination recommendations are provided by Symphony Audition, which combines all of the previously mentioned functions. This information is used as input by the acquisition team to determine the award. Once decided, the award process can be completed by simply adding a contract number from your contract writing system or by using a contract number generated by Symphony.
