Symphony Audition manages solicitation configuration, proposal intake, offer evaluation, source selection and final determinations for your unique RFP criteria. By automating these processes and increasing visibility into industry proposal validations, Symphony Audition enhances proposal quality, reduces protest risk, and simplifies end-to-end solicitation management for your program.

Solicitation Configuration

Symphony Audition is configured to your specific program requirements. Apex Logic will collaborate with your government program team to adapt the Audition tool to meet the needs of your solicitation. The Audition tool also includes a Q&A module that allows you to gather feedback and respond to any questions from industry. Once the solicitation is finalized, Symphony Audition will collect all required information in a standardized format.

Proposal Intake

Offerors use the Symphony Audition product to respond to the solicitation. Audition accounts for the specific needs of your program based on how it was configured. Offerors respond to the solicitation by submitting information in a consistent format, enabling business intelligence and program efficiency. The system provides real-time validation to offerors and displays an error summary page. While Symphony will not reject a submission due to a validation error, it will notify both the program and the offeror of the error.

Offer Evaluation

The Audition module streamlines the evaluation of offeror responses and supporting documentation by organizing the solicitation into manageable sections, promoting consistency, minimizing protest risk, and ensuring fair competition.

Source Selection

Symphony Audition streamlines the source selection process by using your pre-established source selection plan to guarantee fair proposal evaluations. By incorporating well-defined roles, permissions, and validations, the system can alert both offerors and the source selection team of proposal errors. The entire source selection process is meticulously documented in Symphony, culminating in a ranked list of the most qualified offerors that are aligned with your specifications.

Determinations

Symphony Audition consolidates data from all the previous steps and provides final recommendations. These recommendations are used by the acquisition team to decide the contract award. The contract can then be finalized by adding a contract number either from your contract writing system or by using one generated by Symphony. The awarded contract is then ready for receiving task order requests, and all awardee data is automatically accessible in the order management system.
