Symphony has been in use at GSA since 2019 automating the solicitation and task order management for its largest contract vehicles. These include OASIS, ASTRO, Polaris, OASIS+, and Alliant 3.

The Defense Health Agency (DHA) uses Symphony to staff over 400 Medical Treatment Facilities including major military hospitals. Staffing includes physicians, nurses, dentists, ancillary services, and support staff. DHA launched Symphony in 2019 for its Medical Q-Code Services (MQS) contracts. They were in production in 1 month over Christmas Holiday in 2018 and taking orders the first week of January. Medical Support Services (MSS) on-ramped shortly afterwards and more than 1000 orders go through these contracts each year. Since then, using Symphony, they have launched the Omnibus IV Research contract, the Healthcare Environmental Cleaning (HEC) contract, and now, 5 years later, the next generation MQS-NG. All providing a single interface for their buyers.

Other Interesting Projects...

Apex Logic has worked with the HHS OSDBU office for over 7 years. Starting with the design and development of their enterprise system - the Small Business Customer Experience (SBCX). SBCX contains all the features and tools the OSDBU needs to run its efforts in a collaborative environment with ordering activities throughout HHS, SBA, and industry. The feature set includes Small Business Review and Approval workflows, Opportunity Forecasts, Subcontracting Review workflows, and small business outreach features. The jewel of SBCX is its small business directory containing detailed information and capabilities of over 8000 small businesses including up to date SAM and USA Spending data. Apex Logic continues to maintain and operate SBCX while continuing to add new features. Apex Logic is proud of the work we are doing to support small businesses and those responsible for supporting them.
